# Copper Market Outlook 2026: Supply Constraints and Growing Demand

**Published:** January 8, 2026  
**Author:** Metals & Minerals Division

The global copper market is entering a transformative period characterized by supply constraints and unprecedented demand growth driven by the energy transition.

## Supply Side Challenges

### Mining Output Constraints

Major copper-producing regions face mounting challenges:

- **Chile:** Water scarcity affecting major mines
- **Peru:** Social unrest impacting operations
- **DRC:** Infrastructure limitations constraining growth
- **Grade Decline:** Global ore grades continue to deteriorate

### Our Response

Simha Global has strengthened its partnerships with African producers to secure reliable supply streams. Our logistics network now covers key mining regions in Zambia and the DRC, ensuring consistent delivery to Asian markets.

## Demand Drivers

### Electrification Boom

The transition to clean energy is copper-intensive:

| Application | Copper Usage (kg/unit) |
|-------------|------------------------|
| Electric Vehicle | 83 |
| Wind Turbine (MW) | 3,600 |
| Solar Panel (MW) | 2,800 |
| Charging Station | 10 |

### Infrastructure Investment

Global infrastructure spending, particularly in developing economies, continues to drive baseline copper consumption.
